Mission

our team will Produce strives to create a community with sustainable and meaningful values. It does so by providing healthy organic food and right livelihood for its employees.Protecting the local agricultural land base, the environment, and open-pollinated organic seed; and empowering the community through information and education about healthy, local food systems.

Vission

Our Organic Produce will be a nucleus around which a healthy and sustainable community will thrive in the very long term. It will also be an inspiration for the creation and development of other sustainable enterprises that will enhance the community’s health and well-being.
